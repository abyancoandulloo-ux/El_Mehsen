About El Mehsen

With 11+ years of experience, El Mehsen is a celebrated makeup artist and hairdresser based in Mauritius. Known for his signature Arabian style — dramatic smoky eyes, rich blacks, golds, and mauves — he brings a touch of elegance to every bride, editorial, and fashion project.

Beyond makeup, El Mehsen offers hijab styling and custom clothing design, ensuring a complete look for weddings, Eid celebrations, and special events.

“My touch is the Arabian style — I love smokey eyes, with pronounced colors. I use a lot of black, gold, and mauve.” — El Mehsen
